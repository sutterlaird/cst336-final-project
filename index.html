Do You Have Your Go Bag?

A go bag, otherwise referred to as a bug-out bag or a 72-hour bag, is important when disaster strikes. When something bad happens, time is essential and can be the difference between life and death. For example, take the recent wildfires, having a go bag already pre-packed with everything, all you would need to do is grab your bag and go. This would significantly reduce the amount of time you would need to spend in harms way, since instead of needing to gather up all of your items it would all be ready. Another advantage would be that since the bag is already packed with all of the essentials, it becomes easier to make sure you have everything you may need. This is the best option for emergency preperation since it allows you to always be ready to go whenever you may need to.

Food Tips

It is good to keep non-perishable food such as high calorie energy or protein bars. These are good choice since they are lightweight, easy to pack, and easy to eat while moving. Size and weight are important since there will be many items in the bag, so it is best to use limited space, while keeping weight to a minimum. It may also be good to keep gum in your bag since it can not only keep your breath fresh, but it can also help with aleviating hunger.


Tips For Maintaining Your Bag

It is best to have your go bag ready 24/7, since disaster can strike at any time. This means checking your bag every six months to at least change out food and water items. If needed, it is also good to check any medication, and also to make sure that children's clothing in the bag still fits them.
